Privacy Policy for Zara Coach

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zara Coach. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Zara Coach mobile application ("App").

Zara Coach is an educational application designed to help Primary 4-6 and Secondary 1-2 students practice math and English skills with AI-powered personalized feedback.

Important: This app is designed for use by children under parental supervision. Parents or guardians should review this policy and provide consent before allowing children to use the app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Photos of Student Work: When students take photos of their handwritten math work for review and feedback.
  • Typed Answers: Answers entered manually when using the typed input feature.
  • Account Information: Basic profile information if you create an account (name, parent PIN).
  • Voice Input: Voice recordings when using speech recognition features for math practice (processed on-device and not stored).

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Practice session history, problem attempts, scores, and progress metrics.
  • Performance Data: Difficulty levels attempted, time spent on activities, and accuracy rates.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not collect contact information beyond what you provide
  • We do not track browsing behavior outside the app
  • We do not collect identifiable biometric data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Educational Feedback: To analyze student work and provide AI-powered personalized feedback and encouragement.
  • Progress Tracking: To track learning progress and adapt difficulty levels to student performance.
  • Parent Dashboard: To provide parents with insights into their child's learning progress and performance.
  • App Improvement: To improve our AI feedback quality, OCR accuracy, and overall user experience.
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

4.1 Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: Data is securely stored using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with industry-standard encryption.
  • Photos: Student work photos are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage with access restricted to the user's account.
  • Progress Data: Practice history and scores are stored in Firebase Firestore database.

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Firebase security rules restrict data access to authenticated users only
  • Parent dashboard access requires PIN authentication
  • No data is shared with third parties except as described in this policy

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

5.1 Firebase (Google Cloud)

  • Purpose: Authentication, data storage, and cloud services
  • Data Shared: User account information, photos, progress data
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.2 OpenAI (GPT-4 API)

  • Purpose: Generating personalized educational feedback
  • Data Shared: Student answers and problem context (no personally identifiable information)
  • Data Retention: OpenAI does not use data sent via API to train models (as of API terms)
  • Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

5.3 Google ML Kit

  • Purpose: On-device optical character recognition (OCR) for reading handwritten answers
  • Data Processing: Processed on-device; images are not sent to Google servers
  • Privacy Policy: https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Zara Coach is designed for children ages 10-14 (Primary 4 through Secondary 2). We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent
  • Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's data at any time
  • We do not enable public sharing or social features that would expose children's information
  • We do not use children's data for behavioral advertising or marketing
  • We limit data collection to what is necessary for educational purposes

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained as long as the account remains active
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for 2 years may be deleted after notification
  • Practice Sessions: Session history is retained to track long-term progress
  • Photos: Photos of student work are retained until manually deleted by the user

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

8.1 Access and Review

  • View all collected data through the Parent Dashboard
  • Export practice history and progress reports

8.2 Correction and Deletion

  • Delete individual photos or practice sessions
  • Request complete account deletion by contacting us
  • Correct any inaccurate information in the app settings

8.3 Opt-Out

  • Disable voice input features in app settings
  • Stop using the app at any time and request data deletion

9. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Service Providers: With third-party services listed in Section 5, only to operate the app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (users will be notified)

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States where Firebase and OpenAI servers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending a notification through the app for significant changes

Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
